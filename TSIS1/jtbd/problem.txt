The Problem: The "Digital CapEx" Trap.
The Finance & Investment Committee treats software features exactly like aircraft acquisitions.

    2. The Bottleneck: This requires "certainty" before "discovery." Because the organization is designed to buy physical assets (Planes) where the specs are known years in advance, they force that same Waterfall Governance onto software.
